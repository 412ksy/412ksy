Hi, I'm Siyun Kim. I work at the intersection of physical chemistry, computational chemistry, and machine learning, with a focus on data-driven approaches for reaction prediction, reaction exploration, and molecular modeling.

## Research Interests

- ML-assisted computational chemistry
- Reaction prediction and reaction pathway exploration
- Molecular dynamics and DFT acceleration
- Quantum mechanics in chemistry
- Generative models for reaction explanation and chemical design

## Current Focus

- Developing MLIP-based workflows for organic reaction prediction
- Exploring reaction pathways through activation-center enumeration
- Validating reaction mechanisms with xTB, MLIPs, and transition-state analysis
- Building tools for reaction preprocessing and model-reaction extraction
